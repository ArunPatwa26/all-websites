About Wavrik

Wavrik is a passionate startup on a mission to transform how coding is introduced in schools. We're just getting started—but our vision is bold, and our commitment is strong.

Our Vision

At Wavrik, we dream of a future where coding is a core part of every student's learning journey. We're building an educational platform that makes programming accessible, creative, and fun—starting from the ground up.

Our Journey Begins

We may not have students or school partnerships yet, but every big vision starts with a small first step. We're currently designing our curriculum, crafting engaging learning experiences, and reaching out to early partners who share our belief in the power of coding education.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive coding education programs tailored for different age groups and skill levels.

Junior Coders (1st-5th)

Introduction to computational thinking through fun, game-based learning with Scratch and block-based programming.

  • Creative storytelling with code
  • Simple game development
  • Basic animation projects

Middle School (6th-8th)

Transition to text-based coding with Python and web development fundamentals (HTML/CSS/JavaScript).

  • Building simple websites
  • Interactive apps with Python
  • Introduction to robotics

High School (9th-12th)

Advanced programming concepts, data structures, algorithms, and specialized tracks like AI/ML or game development.

  • Full-stack web development
  • Mobile app development
  • Competitive programming

UI/UX Design

Teaching students the principles of design thinking and creating user-friendly interfaces.

  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Color theory and typography
  • User research basics

Creative Tech

Combining coding with art, music, and physical computing for innovative projects.

  • Arduino/Raspberry Pi projects
  • Digital art with code
  • Interactive installations

Teacher Training

Professional development for educators to implement coding in their classrooms.

  • Curriculum integration
  • Classroom resources
  • Ongoing support

Our Goal

At Wavrik, our goal is simple yet bold — to make coding a natural part of every child’s education, no matter their background or prior experience.

Inspire Creativity

We want to spark imagination and curiosity in young minds by teaching them how to bring their ideas to life through code.

Reach Every School

Whether urban or rural, big or small — we aim to partner with schools across the country to ensure no student is left behind.

Build Future Skills

Our curriculum is designed to equip students with real-world tech skills that prepare them for tomorrow’s careers — starting today.
