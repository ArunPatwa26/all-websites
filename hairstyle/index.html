Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially for weekends which tend to fill up quickly. For same-day appointments, please call us to check availability.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

We do accept walk-ins based on availability, but appointments are strongly recommended to guarantee service. Walk-in clients may experience longer wait times during peak hours. You can call ahead to check current wait times.

We follow all local health guidelines, including regular sanitization of tools and workstations, mandatory masks for staff, and optional masks for clients. All tools are sterilized between clients, and we've implemented spaced-out scheduling to reduce crowding.

Yes! We offer physical and digital gift cards in any denomination. They can be purchased in-salon or online and never expire. Gift cards make perfect gifts for birthdays, holidays, or just to show someone you care about their grooming needs.
